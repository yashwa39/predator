How and why we use cookies and similar technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help our site remember your preferences and improve your experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential: required for core functionality (e.g., cookie consent, security)
  • Preferences: remember choices like theme (dark/light)
  • Analytics: help us understand usage and improve the site (optional)

Your Choices

  • You can accept or decline non-essential cookies via our banner
  • Adjust your browser settings to manage cookies
  • Withdraw consent anytime—clear cookies or use the banner
