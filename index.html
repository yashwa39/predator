About PREDATOR

Revolutionizing emergency response through predictive technology

What is PREDATOR?

PREDATOR is an innovative hazard detection and alert system that leverages edge-first processing and mobile device sensors to provide instant, personalized emergency warnings. Our system transforms everyday devices into powerful safety tools, creating a comprehensive network of hazard detection capabilities.

The Three Key Problems We Solve:

Detection Delay

Traditional systems have significant response delays. PREDATOR reduces detection time to under 20 milliseconds.

Limited Reach

Conventional systems have limited coverage. Our edge-first approach ensures comprehensive detection across all areas.

Weak Personalization

Generic alerts lack context. PREDATOR provides personalized warnings based on individual risk profiles and location.
